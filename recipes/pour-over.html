Pour Over

Pour over coffee offers birghter cups of coffee, with minimal body

This recipe will explain how to make a cup of pour over coffee.

ingredients

  • 20 Grams of coffee (pre grounded for pour over)
  • 300 ml of water
  • Kettle for water
  • Pour over tool such as v60 hario
  • Carafe
  • Paper filter
  • Scale

Steps

  1. Fill kettle with plenty of water, more than 300 ml, place on stove and heat until boiling
  2. Place filter in pour over tool, and place pour over tool on top of carafe
  3. Once water is heated, pour hot water into the pour over tool, saturate filter
  4. Dump water out of carafe, and put the pour over tool back onto carafe
  5. Place ground coffee in pour over
  6. Place everything onto scale, and zero scale out
  7. Pour 50 ml of water into coffee bed, let sit for 30 seconds (This is called the bloom, it degases the coffee
  8. Slowly pour in remainder of water (250 ml)
  9. Once water is filtered through, your drink is ready, pour coffee into a mug
