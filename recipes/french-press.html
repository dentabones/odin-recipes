French Press

French press offers more body to coffee, while sacrificing brightness

This recipe explains how to make french press coffee. This is one of the more accessible ways to make coffee

    Ingredients:
  • 30 grams of coffee
  • 500 ml of water
  • Kettle
  • French Press
    Steps:
  1. Boil 500 ml of water
  2. Place coffee in french presss
  3. pour water into french press
  4. Let sit for 4 minutes
  5. Push the french press plunger down, pour coffee into mug, ready to drink!
